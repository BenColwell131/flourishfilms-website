      <!-- Paragraph 1: Project overview -->
      <h3>Project:</h3>
      <p>Previous work with RTÉ on the Directors in Dialogue series introduced the Flourish team to the RTÉ creative crew and initiated another project for RTÉ which was produced throughout the Summer of 2018. RTÉ wanted to produce a series of 40 short clips for release on social media and as pre-roll ads on YouTube. They needed Flourish to come up with the ideas and fashion together the creative. </p>

      <!-- Paragraph 2: Strategy -->
      <h3>Strategy:</h3>
      <p>The project involved bulk filming of lifehacks across a number of days on a set designed for efficient production, using an overhead camera setup and a coloured background. The lifehacks were turned around quickly and produced into a package with a coherent and consistent theme, making them easily identifiable to viewers, concise and informative. </p>
